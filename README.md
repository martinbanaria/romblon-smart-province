## Platform Summaries

### Gyud.AI — Digital Governance & Citizen Clarity

**Purpose:** Filipino-made AI platform for local government operations

**For Councillors & Policy-Makers:**
- Instant ordinance search across all resolutions, committee reports
- AI identifies amendments, penalties, implementing offices
- Policy interconnection analysis

**For LGU Departments:**
- Centralized dashboards (budgets, contracts, disbursements)
- Automated anomaly detection & compliance alerts
- Standardized workflows reducing manual coordination

**For Citizens:**
- Natural language queries in Filipino/English/local dialects
- 24/7 access via web, mobile, SMS
- Clear answers on permits, taxes, penalties

---

### EdTech LMS — From Learning to Earning

**Purpose:** Career-first learning platform with AI-powered pathways to employment

**Career Pathways:**
- AI-powered skills assessment & career mapping
- Personalized learning paths based on job goals
- Direct job matching with local & remote employers
- Post-placement success tracking

**Tech & AI Skills (Industry-Agnostic):**
- AI Fundamentals: Prompt engineering, AI tools for work
- Digital Skills: Google Workspace, Canva, basic coding
- Data & Analytics: Excel, SQL basics, dashboarding
- Remote Work Ready: Virtual collaboration, freelancing

**Industry-Specific Tracks:**
- Tourism: Hospitality tech, booking systems, guest experience
- Marble: CAD/CAM basics, quality control, export standards
- BPO: English proficiency, typing, customer service
- Government: eGov systems, citizen service, data privacy

## Technical Highlights

**Compliance by Design:**
- Data Privacy Act (RA 10173) — DPIA-compliant
- DICT Cloud First Policy (2020)
- EODB Act (RA 11032) workflows
- DepEd Digital Transformation Roadmap
- COA audit trail requirements

**Deployment Flexibility:**
- On-premise (LGU-managed servers)
- Hybrid cloud (Philippine data centers)
- Full data sovereignty & custody
- Offline-capable modules
- SMS/low-bandwidth access modes

**Accessibility:**
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliant color contrast
- Full keyboard navigation
- Screen reader compatible
- Touch-friendly mobile targets
